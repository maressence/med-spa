FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Is laser hair removal safe for dark skin? ⌄
Yes — our Alma Soprano Titanium laser is specifically safe and effective for all skin tones, including South Asian, Middle Eastern, Hispanic, and African skin. Unlike older laser technologies, it uses gradual heating rather than high-intensity pulses, making it comfortable and safe for darker complexions.
How many laser sessions will I need? ⌄
Most clients need 6–8 sessions for permanent hair reduction, spaced 4–6 weeks apart. The exact number depends on your hair type, color, and the area being treated. Your aesthetician will assess this during your free consultation.
What is Alma TED and how does it work? ⌄
Alma TED (Transepidermal Drug Delivery) uses ultrasound energy and air pressure to deliver active hair growth ingredients deep into the scalp — completely needle-free. It's a non-surgical alternative to PRP injections for hair thinning and loss, with no downtime and no discomfort.
What is BioRePeel and is there downtime? ⌄
BioRePeel is a next-generation chemical peel that stimulates collagen, brightens skin, and treats acne — without the peeling or downtime of traditional peels. Most clients return to normal activities immediately. It's ideal for all skin types.
Where are you located and how do I enter? ⌄
We're located at 341 Jerusalem Ave, Hicksville, NY 11801 — inside MZF Medical. Please enter through the MZF Medical entrance. Customer parking is available on-site.
Do you offer consultations? ⌄
Yes — we offer free consultations. This is a great opportunity to discuss your goals, ask questions, and let our aesthetician recommend the right treatment plan for your skin type and concerns. Book via phone or online.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Zelle. Payment is collected at the time of service.
How do I prepare for my laser session? ⌄
Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment. Avoid sun exposure, self-tanner, and waxing for at least 2 weeks prior. Come to your appointment with clean, product-free skin. Your aesthetician will provide full pre-care instructions at your consultation.
